Delete Account

Mammon: The Gilded Rot

You can permanently delete your account and all associated data directly inside the app — no email or form required.

How to delete your account in-app

  1. Open Mammon: The Gilded Rot.
  2. From the town screen, tap the Settings icon (gear icon in the navigation bar).
  3. Scroll to the Account section.
  4. Tap Delete Account.
  5. Confirm the deletion when prompted.

Deletion is immediate and permanent. The following data will be removed:

  • Your Firebase authentication account (email, Google, or Apple ID link)
  • All cloud-saved character progress and game data
Note: If you signed in a long time ago, Firebase may require you to sign out and sign in again before deletion is allowed (a security measure). After re-signing in, repeat the steps above.

Need help?

If you are unable to delete your account from within the app, contact us at thanhbt001@gmail.com and we will delete your account and data within 30 days.
