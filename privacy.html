MAMMON Privacy Policy

Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 23, 2026

Overview

Mammon RPG ("the App") is developed and operated by Thanh Bui Tuan ("we", "us", or "our"). This Privacy Policy describes how we handle information in connection with your use of the App.

Data We Do Not Collect

Mammon RPG is an offline game. We do not collect, transmit, or store any personal information. Specifically, we do not collect:

  • Your name, email address, or contact details
  • Location data
  • Device identifiers or advertising IDs
  • Usage analytics or gameplay telemetry
  • Crash reports sent to our servers
  • Any financial or payment information

Local Storage

All game data (your save file, settings, and progress) is stored entirely on your device using local storage. This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us.

Third-Party Services

The App is built using Expo (a React Native framework). The App does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking SDKs.

Apple may collect certain device and usage data as part of operating the App Store and iOS platform. Please refer to Apple's Privacy Policy for details.

Children's Privacy

The App is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect any personal information from children. Since we collect no personal data at all, there is no risk of inadvertent data collection from minors.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
