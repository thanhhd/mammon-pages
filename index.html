MAMMON

Dark Action RPG

A world corrupted by greed

Mammon is a dark action RPG set in Aurelia — a city decaying under the grip of Mammon, Prince of Greed. Play as a Sorceress, hunt for legendary loot, and unravel a conspiracy that runs deeper than anyone suspects.

Deep Diablo-style item system. Strategic turn-based combat. Rich narrative with branching quests and faction choices.
