Frequently Asked Questions

The app crashes on launch

Try force-closing the app and reopening it. If the issue persists, delete and reinstall the app. Your progress is saved locally — reinstalling may reset your save file.

I lost my save data

Save data is stored locally on your device. Deleting the app will delete your save. We recommend not deleting the app unless necessary. Cloud save is planned for a future update.

A skill or item isn't working correctly

Please report the specific skill name, item name, and what happened vs what you expected. Screenshots are very helpful.

The game is too hard / too easy

Difficulty scaling is actively being tuned. Please share your feedback — which zone, which enemy, and your character level at the time.

I found a bug

Please report bugs with as much detail as possible: what you were doing, what happened, and your device model and iOS version.
